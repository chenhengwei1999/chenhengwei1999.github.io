Research Direction

Safety of the Intended Functionality (SOTIF)

Since I entered graduate school, the direction of my research has been changing. Changes that are mainly related to autonomous driving, but not very explicit research. If I had to pick one, my master's thesis was related to safety of intended function (SOTIF), but without a very clear research component. SOTIF focuses on safety issues related to self-driving vehicles due to design flaws, inadequate functionality, or human misuse.

Cybersceurity

During the first year of my master's degree, my research was on information security (cybersecurity) for Telematics, which was focused on the design of intrusion detection system (IDS) for automotive CAN bus data. Later, the work on cybersecurity was interrupted when the group applied for SOTIF project before the start of the master's degree.
